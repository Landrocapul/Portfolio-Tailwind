ABOUT ME

Nice that you have found me. I am a student of Information Technology, focusing on web and mobile development with knowledge in PHP, JavaScript, Java, C++, and Android development.

I began as the editor of our school’s ICT Club and later joined our family sublimation business as a layout artist, designing logos, jerseys, and t-shirts. These roles helped me connect creativity with technology.

I also have experience in video editing and skills in Photoshop, CorelDRAW, and Illustrator, which I use for logo and apparel design. Alongside my studies, I work on freelance projects to sharpen my craft and gain real-world experience.

When I’m not coding or designing, I explore new tools, build personal projects, and spend time with family and friends.
